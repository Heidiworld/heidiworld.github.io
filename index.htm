Heidiworld is the online home of Heidi Crook A Merseyside based Front-End Developer and sometime illustrator

I hate to admit how long I've been building websites because it might reveal my true age, but it all started in 1998. Since then the "web" has changed a lot. It's a good job I embrace change and love learning new things.

I have worked for clients such as Marks and Spencer, Bentley, Jaguar Landrover, NHS, Tarmac, Vodafone, Health & Safety Executive and United Utilities.

I trained to be be an illustrator. I became a web designer. Then, 10 years later, I gave in and embraced my inner nerd. I now completely focus on writing and crafting beautiful html, css and javascript.

My goal is create accessible, bullet proof, maintainable and extensible code. Quality and love for what I deliver are my driving principles.

Every visitor to your website deserves the best possible experience independent of disability, device, browser, bandwidth, and if there is a Tuesday in the week. It's not ok to discriminate.

I am not an island. I love working with others and have worked with developers specialising on quite a number different platforms and content management systems including Sitecore, Umbraco, Sharepoint, Open Text (Red Dot) and Episerver.

All of which have their own unique Front-End challenges. It's a lot of fun.

Recent projects include: Robinsons Brewery and LIPA 4:19.

Anyway I hope to present a far more exciting website here in the future, but... I'm having so much fun at Mando Group my own website gets neglected. Feel free to follow me on the various social medias if you wish to stay in contact.
